Aromas of kitchen and medicine!
A delight smell and effective healing essences arise from the aromatic plants in this garden. 
Thyme and bay, basil and cumin, pepper and sweet pepper, clove and anise, rosemary and parsley, coriander and bergamot, cardamom, vanilla and the famous cinnamon are represented in kitchens all over the world. 
Equally well represented in the alchemy are lavender, mint, and eucalyptus. Ever since these plants have been used to treat diseases, in beauty products for centuries and the worship of gods. 

The power of aroma is, was and will always be healing, subtle and profound. 
Enjoy this garden full of aromas!
