The totora . It can be found in almost every household.

Totora is a form of reed originating from south America.
The stem of this plant consists of resistant and flexible fibers, which are excellent to manufacture into mats and boats. 

She grows in swamps and at the borders of Andean lakes and lagoons. 

A good mattress.
The natives from the region of Quito have been manufacturing mats from totora since the antique. Sometimes they were used as beds and sometimes just as a tablecloth. The people from the Lake Titicaca are famous for their totora constructions, such as fisher boats and even houses.
