Orchids, the first natural heritage of Ecuador!

1.    Orchids are champions of the vegetal biodiversity in Ecuador. One out of five plants is an Orchid.
2.    In Ecuador, you can find 4.032 classified species of orchids and more than 400 in process of determination.
1.710 species are endemic, which means that they are unique in the way that you can only find them in one spot and nowhere else.
3. Ecuador is the country with the highest biodiversity on the planet. The species of orchids is the most numerous with 37,9% of endemics within all vascular plants.
